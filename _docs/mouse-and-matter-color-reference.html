Usage Rules
Amber is always present
#D4952A is the heartbeat of the brand. Every asset should have it somewhere — a label, a rule, an accent. Never absent.
Max three colors per asset
Pick a background, a text color, and an accent. Do not use the full palette in a single Canva graphic or Instagram post.
Signal green is supporting only
#8AAB72 never leads. Use it as a third accent at most — a small tag, a divider, a data point. Never the dominant color.
The & is always gold
In the wordmark and anywhere the brand name appears with color available, the ampersand is always #D4952A.
