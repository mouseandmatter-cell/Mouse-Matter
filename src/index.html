The Selector

How We Work

The first thing we ask is simple.
Tell me about your product.

Not the pitch. Not the deck. Just tell us what it does and who it helps in your own words. Because how you answer that question tells us everything we need to know about where the real work begins.

What we are listening for is not just the strategy gaps. We are listening for the vision that has not been fully named yet. The audience that has not been fully seen. The version of your product that is bigger than the one you have been pitching. Most teams have been so close to what they built that they can only see one version of it. We create the space to think bigger. Then we build the structure, the messaging, the go to market motion, and the team alignment that turns that bigger vision into something the market can actually feel.

Most PMMs come in and optimize what exists. We come in and help you see what is possible. That is where we start. And it starts the moment you say tell me about your product.

The Work

This is not a consulting engagement.
This is your PMM function.

Mouse & Matter embeds into your business the way a senior PMM Director would. We are in your customer success conversations. We are sitting with your sales team understanding where deals stall. We are talking to product about what the market needs next. And we are building the materials, the messaging, and the strategy that ties all of it together. Every week. Not as a vendor. As a partner who owns the go to market motion alongside you.

01
Listen to the Market

We start where most PMMs skip. Customer success conversations. Sales calls. Win loss analysis. What people say is broken. What they love. What they wish existed. We sit in those conversations consistently because the market is always talking and most companies are not listening closely enough. The signal is there. We find it and turn it into strategy.

02
Read the Room

We track what your competitors are doing, where they are showing up, how they talk about their product, and where their gaps are. We build and continuously refine the personas that reflect who your audience actually is, not just their job title but their full life and what they need right now. Most teams have been inside their product so long they can only see one version of it. We pull you out of the tunnel and up to the full view.

03
Connect with Product

We sit with your product team regularly. Not to report what marketing needs. But to bring them what the market is saying and help shape what gets built next. New launches, feature prioritization, positioning for what is coming. We make sure product and go to market are never moving in different directions. That alignment is where growth actually comes from.

04
Enable the Team

All that research, all that listening, all that cross-functional work has to turn into something the team can actually use. The messaging framework that gives everyone the same language. The sales materials that answer the objections before they come up. The website content and webinars that bring the right buyers in and start the conversation before anyone picks up the phone. The conference presence that makes your brand impossible to ignore in the rooms that matter. We build all of it. And we build it so your team can run it confidently without us needing to be in every room.
